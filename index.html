Opportunity Criteria

Opportunity Criteria

At Trident, we don’t follow trends, we follow data, fundamentals, and long-term value. Below are the core criteria we use to evaluate every opportunity:

1

Historical Profitability

We target businesses with a proven track record of positive cash flow, healthy margins, and consistent financial performance.

2

Clear Value Proposition

The businesses we back must offer a compelling solution to a real problem.

3

Defensible Market Position

Trident invests in companies with strong competitive moats.

4

Stable Revenue Streams

Predictability is powerful. We prioritize businesses with recurring or highly repeatable revenue models.
